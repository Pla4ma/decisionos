# DecisionOS — Tester Feedback Questions

## Purpose

Structured questions to guide private testers through providing comprehensive feedback on DecisionOS MVP.

---

## Core Questions (Required)

### Decision Flow Experience

**1. Was the decision flow clear?**
- Did you understand what information was needed at each step?
- Were the guided questions helpful for thinking through your decision?
- Any parts of the creation process that felt confusing or unnecessary?
- Suggestions for improving the flow?

### Analysis Quality

**2. Did the analysis feel useful?**
- Did the AI analysis help you see your decision differently?
- Were the insights specific to your situation or generic?
- Did the analysis change how you thought about your options?
- What was most/least helpful about the analysis?

### Score System

**3. Did the scores make sense?**
- Do the 5 dimensions (confidence, regret risk, values alignment, reversibility, risk) feel relevant?
- Were the score explanations clear and helpful?
- Did the scores help you compare your options?
- Any scoring dimensions that felt missing or unnecessary?

### AI Quality Assessment

**4. Did anything feel generic like ChatGPT?**
- Did the responses feel tailored to your specific decision?
- Any responses that seemed like generic AI advice?
- What made it feel more/less personalized?
- Examples of particularly good or bad AI responses?

### Safety and Trust

**5. Did anything feel unsafe or overconfident?**
- Any advice that felt too certain or risky?
- Did the AI acknowledge uncertainty appropriately?
- Any responses that felt like medical, legal, or investment advice?
- Your comfort level with the AI's confidence level?

### Real-World Application

**6. Would you use this for a real decision?**
- What type of decisions would you actually use this for?
- What would prevent you from using it for important decisions?
- Would you trust the recommendations for real-life choices?
- In what situations would this be most valuable?

### Monetization Value

**7. What would make you pay?**
- Are the Plus features compelling enough to justify payment?
- What price point would feel reasonable?
- Would you prefer one-time payment or subscription?
- What additional features would make it worth paying for?

### Usability Issues

**8. What confused you?**
- Where did you get stuck or need help?
- Any unclear buttons, labels, or navigation?
- Features you expected but couldn't find?
- Technical issues or bugs encountered?

## Feedback Format Guidelines

### How to Provide Feedback
- **Be specific**: Instead of "it was confusing," say "the score explanations were unclear because..."
- **Include examples**: Quote specific responses or describe exact situations
- **Separate issues**: Technical bugs vs feature suggestions vs user experience issues
- **Be honest**: Both positive and negative feedback are valuable

### What Helps Most
- **Concrete examples** of good/bad experiences
- **Comparison** to your current decision-making process
- **Context** about your decision-making style and needs
- **Suggestions** for specific improvements

### What to Avoid
- Vague generalities without examples
- Features outside the current MVP scope
- Comparisons to enterprise tools (unless relevant)
- Personal information about your actual decisions
